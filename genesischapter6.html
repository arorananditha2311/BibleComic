📖 Genesis Chapter 6

Simple Explanation

Genesis Chapter 6 describes the increasing wickedness of humanity and God’s sorrow over their evil actions. It also introduces Noah, who found favor with God. God reveals His plan to bring a flood to cleanse the earth, but He instructs Noah to build an ark to save his family and representatives of all living creatures. This chapter emphasizes God’s justice, mercy, and the beginning of His covenant with Noah.
