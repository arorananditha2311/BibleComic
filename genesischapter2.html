📖 Genesis Chapter 2

Simple Explanation

Genesis Chapter 2 provides a more detailed view of God’s creation, focusing on the creation of humankind and the Garden of Eden. God formed man from the dust and breathed life into him. God planted a beautiful garden in Eden, with rivers and trees, including the tree of life and the tree of knowledge of good and evil. God created woman from man’s rib, establishing the first human relationship. This chapter emphasizes God’s care, order, and the beginning of human relationships.
