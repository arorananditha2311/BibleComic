📖 Genesis Chapter 1

Simple Explanation

Genesis Chapter 1 describes the story of God’s creation of the world in six days. God created light, sky, land, seas, plants, sun, moon, stars, animals, and finally, humankind. On the seventh day, God rested, making it holy. This chapter shows God’s power, order, and purpose in creation.
