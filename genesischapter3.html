📖 Genesis Chapter 3

Simple Explanation

Genesis Chapter 3 tells the story of the Fall of Man. The serpent tempted Eve to eat from the tree of knowledge of good and evil, and she shared the fruit with Adam. Their eyes were opened to good and evil, and they realized they were naked. God confronted them, and as a consequence, humanity was expelled from the Garden of Eden. This chapter highlights the beginning of human sin, disobedience, and the need for redemption.
