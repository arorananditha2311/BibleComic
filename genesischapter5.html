📖 Genesis Chapter 5

Simple Explanation

Genesis Chapter 5 provides a genealogy from Adam to Noah, tracing the descendants and ages of each generation. It highlights the long lifespans of the early humans and God’s continuing plan for humanity through the line of Seth, Adam’s son after Abel’s death. This chapter emphasizes God’s faithfulness and the continuity of His creation across generations.
