📖 Genesis Chapter 10

Simple Explanation

Genesis Chapter 10 is called the "Table of Nations." It lists the descendants of Noah’s sons—Shem, Ham, and Japheth— and the nations that emerged from them after the flood. This chapter shows how humanity spread across the earth and highlights the origins of many ancient peoples. It emphasizes God’s plan and the continuity of human life after the flood.
