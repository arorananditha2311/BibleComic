📖 Genesis Chapter 9

Simple Explanation

Genesis Chapter 9 describes God’s covenant with Noah after the flood. God blesses Noah and his sons, giving them authority over all living creatures and commanding them to multiply and fill the earth. God establishes the rainbow as a sign of His promise that He will never again destroy all life with a flood. This chapter highlights God’s mercy, faithfulness, and the new beginning for humanity.
