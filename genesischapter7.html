📖 Genesis Chapter 7

Simple Explanation

Genesis Chapter 7 details the beginning of the Great Flood. God instructs Noah to enter the ark with his family and all kinds of animals. For forty days and forty nights, rain falls upon the earth, covering it completely. This chapter highlights God’s judgment against sin and His protection of Noah and the creatures in the ark.
